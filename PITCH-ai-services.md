## SLIDE 5 — The 6 Automations That Change Your Operation

**Headline:**
Here's What We Actually Build for Firms Like Yours

**Subheadline:** Each one is a specific workflow — not a generic "AI tool."

---

**1. Subcontractor Compliance Monitoring**
- Continuously tracks insurance certs, license renewals, and OSHA certifications across your entire sub list.
- Sends automatic alerts 60 and 30 days before anything expires.
- *Result: No more last-minute scrambles. No more project exposure.*

---

**2. Change Order Pipeline**
- Every change event — whether it starts in the field, via SMS, or from a site video — flows through a structured workflow.
- From field notification to owner approval, every PCO is logged, costed, and documented automatically.
- *Result: No more lost change orders. No more unlogged T&M leaking out of your margin.*

---

**3. Owner Progress Reporting**
- Weekly project status reports — schedule, budget variance, open RFIs — formatted and delivered to owners and stakeholders automatically.
- Your PMs don't write a single line of it.
- *Result: Owners stay informed. PMs stay on the job.*

---

**4. Bid Management & RFQ Tracking**
- RFQs go out to your sub list automatically. Deadline reminders go out automatically. Bid summaries come back to your inbox — organized and ready to level.
- *Result: No more managing 40 email threads per bid cycle.*

---

**5. Project Kickoff Workflow**
- The moment a contract executes, a structured sequence fires: NTP issued, sub setup packets sent, document folders created, schedule distributed.
- *Result: Every project starts clean, on day one, without a PM manually coordinating six things at once.*

---

**6. Subcontractor Intake & Pre-Qualification**
- New subs apply through a structured pipeline. Trade credentials, insurance, and references are collected and organized before they ever touch a job.
- *Result: Your approved sub list stays current and qualified — automatically.*

---

**Speaker note:**
Walk through these one at a time. Ask after each: "Is this something that takes time on your team right now?" You want him to self-identify which two or three matter most. That's your pilot scope.
